Full model fine-tuning requires massive GPU memory. Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) freezes the base parameters, saving resources.

We compare different PEFT approaches, evaluating their memory footprints and target modules.

This allows us to train models on standard developer rigs instead of multi-GPU servers.
