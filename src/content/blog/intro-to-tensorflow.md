TensorFlow is a powerhouse for deep learning. Multi-Layer Perceptrons (MLPs) represent the basic structure of fully-connected neural networks.

In this guide, we examine backpropagation, loss functions, and optimization. We configure layers and dense units to handle tabular regression tasks.

By training on structured datasets, we analyze epoch output, check validation loss, and implement early stopping to avoid overfitting.
