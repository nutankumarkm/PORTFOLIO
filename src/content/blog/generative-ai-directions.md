Generative AI has evolved rapidly, moving from Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and GANs to modern self-attention Transformer blocks.

We explore attention mechanisms, encoder-decoder architectures, and why modern LLMs scale so efficiently with compute.

Finally, we discuss context windows, prompt formatting, and task-specific fine-tuning trends in 2026.
